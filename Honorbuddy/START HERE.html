New to Honorbuddy?
Honorbuddy does not run in a vacuum—it depends on certain infrastructure components to be available on your system. If you installed Honorbuddy using the .exe file, the components should be acquired and installed for you, if they are missing. If you installed Honorbuddy using the .zip file, there is a chance some of the necessary components may be missing on your system. If so, you must run this tool with Windows administration privileges to have the dependencies installed:
Buddy Dependency Wizard

If you have problems getting Honorbuddy to function...
If you have a problem, the Honorbuddy Helpdesk should be your first stop:
Honorbuddy HelpDesk

If the HelpDesk is unable to help you resolve your problem...
please make a new thread in the Support forum. Support threads are dedicated to you and your problem—users other than you and the Bossland GmbH staff will be unable to post in your Support thread. The Support team will work with you in this thread to resolve your problem. The BosslandGmbH Support staff monitors the Support forum several times a day—other parts of the BosslandGmbH forums are examined much less frequently.

Most people don't report bugs frequently. As such, they don't know what to say, or what kind of information is needed by the Support staff. This article should get the necessary information to the Support staff to address the problem as quickly as possible:
Asking for Support / Reporting a bug

Anytime you ask for help anywhere in the forum, be certain to attach the full unedited log that demonstrates the problem.

Updating Honorbuddy...
Honorbuddy has a built-in updater, and it will provide you with an option to upgrade any time a new version is available. Much of the time, updating is optional, but it is always a wise choice. Updates make certain your bot has the latest bug fixes, and your WoWaccount is maximally protected. Occasionally, updating is required when the WoWclient has made significant changes to the extent that the existing Buddy bot version will no longer work.

You can always find the latest versions of the Buddy bots here:
